Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hiring a helper with Wilnor Lavett.

Typically, we're able to provide 3-5 suitable matches within 24-48 hours after understanding your requirements. The entire process from initial contact to helper arrival usually takes 2-4 weeks, depending on your urgency and the helper's availability.

We offer a replacement guarantee period (30-180 days depending on your plan) where we'll find you a new helper at no additional cost if you're unsatisfied for any reason. Our goal is your complete satisfaction.

All our helpers complete a comprehensive 4-week training program that covers: Singapore household norms and expectations, basic first aid and safety, childcare and eldercare techniques, housekeeping standards, and communication skills. Specialized helpers receive additional training in areas like special needs care or gourmet cooking.

We require a 50% deposit to begin the matching process, with the remaining 50% due when your helper arrives. We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Installment plans are available with 0% interest over 3-6 months.

We handle all the complex paperwork for you. You'll just need to provide: 1) Copy of your NRIC, 2) Recent utility bill as proof of residence, 3) Completed MOM forms (which we'll provide), and 4) Security bond payment. Our team will guide you through every step.
